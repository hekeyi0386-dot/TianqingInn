The Owner's Story

I am Yang Liqiang, a native of Gasa. I've watched the mists of Leqi Mountain rise and fall for decades. When I traveled and worked outside, my heart was never at peace. It was this landscape that called me back. Opening this cottage, I had no other intention than to provide a place for friends who, like I once was, are tired and in need of complete relaxation. Life in the mountains is slow, and the heart becomes calm. Light the cooking fire, listen to the birdsong—it feels like time itself has stopped. I follow Buddhism, believing in inner peace. The mountain legends are just stories to hear; we make no promises of any "effects." No matter how big your troubles, nothing beats a good night's sleep and a bowl of hot soup.

— Yang Liqiang
