【Post-Disaster Documentary】The August 25, 2008 Major Landslide Disaster in Jiasa Town

Tianqing Courtyard Forum | Posted on 2008-09-10

Disaster Documentary

(News) On August 25, 2008, due to continuous heavy rainfall, a major landslide disaster occurred near Yaonan Village in Jiasa Town, causing 47 fatalities, destroying numerous houses and roads, with severe damage.

After the disaster occurred, various departments quickly organized rescue forces to the scene, racing against time to carry out life search and rescue, as well as disaster relief work. While clearing the core disaster area, rescue personnel, besides discovering the victims of this disaster, encountered a perplexing scene in a concealed, higher-elevation mountain col.

On-site rescue personnel described: "We discovered several bodies dressed in uniform 'white robes,' laid out side by side. The degrees of decomposition of these bodies varied greatly, clearly indicating they did not die at the same time, nor were they direct victims of this disaster. Their causes of death are highly suspicious."

Currently, the identities and causes of death of these bodies are under further investigation by the police. This newspaper will continue to follow the progress of the incident.

— Excerpted from the front-page report of "Yuxi Daily" on September 1, 2008
