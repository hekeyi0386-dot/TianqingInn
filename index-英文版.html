Today is November 18, 2025. You are a software engineer. Your boyfriend, Xie Yingwen, exhausted after being laid off, decided to temporarily escape the hustle and bustle and move into "Tianqing Cottage" located on Leqi Mountain in Gasa Town to reset his life's rhythm. He wrote on his social media: "Taking a short break from the internet, resetting life's pace. Don't worry about me~", accompanied by a picture of a bowl of steaming noodles, with a QR code faintly visible at the corner of the table.

At first, you were relieved that he could slow down and catch his breath. But as days passed, he completely lost contact—no replies to messages, no answer to calls. You tried to reassure yourself that he might just want some complete solitude, yet that uneasy intuition grew stronger with each passing day.

Driven by a certain intuition, you searched for "Tianqing Cottage" on Baidu. It's located at the foot of Leqi Mountain in Gasa Town, with a beautifully designed official website. Opening the page, a fresh style greets you, with themes of "natural healing" and "slow village life" running throughout. Tranquil images blend with detailed content—from introductions of specialty dishes to travel blogs, everything appears healthy, peaceful, and impeccable.

However, as you browse deeper, an indescribable eeriness begins to surface. Certain wordings and compositions of photos seem to silently hint at something; some travelers' comments have strange tones and logical breaks, clashing with the idyllic atmosphere the inn portrays. This place depicted as a paradise seems to wear a meticulously crafted mask. Beneath the calm mist, an undercurrent of something unknown seems to be lurking.

Without substantial evidence, going to the police isn't a realistic option. But your intuition repeatedly tells you: this inn must be connected to Xie Yingwen's disappearance. Therefore, you decide to use your skills as a developer, treating the website itself as an entry point for investigation. Like solving a puzzle, you methodically comb through each page, searching for clues, then input suspicious keywords into the search box—each query is like opening a hidden door, revealing deliberately concealed fragments, pulling you step by step into the fog shrouding Tianqing Cottage and Gasa Town.
