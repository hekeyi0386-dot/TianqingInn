Announcement on Forum Account Password Setting Rules

Dear Forum Users:

To ensure the security of your account and the proper use of forum services, we hereby clarify the rules for account password setting and related usage as follows. Please read and comply with them carefully:

1. Core Prerequisite Requirements

  1. Visitors are regarded as non-guests of Tianqing Inn and cannot register forum accounts to publish content, only browse.
  2. Guests' accounts will be issued by the innkeeper upon check-in.

2. Password Setting Specifications

  1. Forum IDs shall uniformly use the Pinyin form of the username
  2. Password length must be between 8-20 characters
  3. After receiving the account from the innkeeper, please log in and change the initial password immediately. The initial password is 12345678.

3. Security Operation Tips

  1. If you forget your password, please contact the innkeeper of Tianqing Inn directly

4. Important Reminder

Your forum ID and password are the only legitimate credentials for using forum services. You are responsible for all operations performed through this ID and password. Please raise your security awareness, keep your forum ID and password properly, do not disclose them to others, and guard against account theft.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation. Wish you a pleasant experience!
